How it works

  • Set your zip — we pull current weekly ads for your local stores.
  • Search by item name across every retailer at once.
  • Star your favorites; we push when peak markdown days approach.
  • Tap "Shop" on any deal to open it at the retailer's site.

How we stay free

When you tap "Shop" on a deal and buy from the retailer, we may earn a small commission at no cost to you. It never changes which deals we show or how they're ranked. That's it — no subscriptions, no ads in your face, no data resold to advertisers.
