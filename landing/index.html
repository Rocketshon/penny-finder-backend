The features that matter

01

AI item categorization

Every deal is auto-classified by Claude into 12 buckets — tools, beauty, food, pet, tech, apparel, etc. Browse "what's on sale in beauty" across every retailer in one tap.

02

Scan-to-check UPC

Point your camera at a price tag — instant haptic feedback for a 1¢ match. Logs the find to your ledger, prefills resale-comp lookups for flippers.

03

Best time to visit

Live foot-traffic forecasts for every store. The app tells you which DG to hit at 7am Tuesday before the markdown shelf gets picked over.

04

Receipt scanner with cross-store comparison

Snap a receipt — Claude Vision parses every line item and shows what you could've saved by buying it elsewhere. Plus referral codes for Fetch + Ibotta to stack rewards on top.

05

Pair Sync for hunting partners

Share watchlist + finds + live store status across two devices in real time. Couples + flipping partners can split the route and never miss a confirmed UPC.

06

AI resale estimates

Pro Mode unlocks Claude-powered eBay sold-range estimates per UPC. "Worth flipping?" answered in 3 seconds before you walk to the register.
